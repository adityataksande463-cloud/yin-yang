1. Watch the Breath

Observe the natural flow of your breathing without controlling it.

  1. Sit comfortably with spine erect.
  2. Close your eyes and take two deep breaths.
  3. Breathe normally and bring attention to nostrils.
  4. Feel air coming in (cool) and going out (warm).
  5. Simply watch your breath. Do not change it.
  6. Continue for 5–15 minutes.

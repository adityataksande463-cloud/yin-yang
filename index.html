Meditation Techniques

1. Watch the Breath

Observe the natural flow of your breathing.

  1. Sit comfortably with spine erect.
  2. Close your eyes and take two deep breaths.
  3. Breathe normally and bring attention to nostrils.
  4. Feel air coming in (cool) and going out (warm).
  5. Simply watch your breath. Do not change it.
  6. Continue for 5–15 minutes.

2. Observe Thoughts

Watch thoughts appear and disappear.

  1. Sit comfortably and close your eyes.
  2. Notice thoughts appearing in the mind.
  3. Do not follow or judge them.
  4. Simply observe them passing by.
  5. Return attention to your breath if mind wanders.
  6. Continue for 5–15 minutes.

3. The Inner Witness

Become aware of the observer behind the mind.

  1. Sit comfortably.
  2. Close your eyes and breathe deeply.
  3. Imagine yourself observing your mind.
  4. Do not interfere with thoughts.
  5. Stay as the observer for 5–15 minutes.

4. Listening Meditation

Listen to sounds without judgment.

  1. Find a quiet space.
  2. Close your eyes and focus on external sounds.
  3. Shift attention to inner sounds.
  4. Do not label or analyze.
  5. Observe for 5–15 minutes.

5. Breath Pause

Observe the pause between inhale and exhale.

  1. Sit comfortably with eyes closed.
  2. Focus on natural breathing.
  3. Notice the pause at the end of exhale.
  4. Do not hold the breath, just observe.
  5. Continue 5–15 minutes.

6. Body Awareness

Scan your body sensations without judgment.

  1. Close your eyes and focus on your feet.
  2. Move attention slowly up the body.
  3. Notice each body part without judgment.
  4. Reverse and repeat as needed.

7. Watching Emotions

Observe emotions like passing clouds.

  1. Notice an emotion arise.
  2. Observe physical sensations.
  3. Do not act or judge.
  4. Feel the emotion pass naturally.

8. Silent Sitting

Sit in stillness and observe inner calm.

  1. Find a comfortable position.
  2. Close eyes, breathe slowly.
  3. Focus on stillness, do not move.
  4. Stay aware for 5–15 minutes.

9. Walking Awareness

Walk slowly, feeling each step.

  1. Walk slowly in a quiet space.
  2. Feel each step and foot movement.
  3. Notice sensations in legs and body.
  4. Keep attention on movement and balance.

10. Cosmic Awareness

Feel yourself as part of the universe.

  1. Close your eyes.
  2. Imagine the vast cosmos around you.
  3. Feel the connection between yourself and universe.
  4. Observe sensations without judgment.
